Agarose gel electrophoresis

Agarose gel was used during practical laboratory sessions and during my internship in the context of protein extraction and purification. It was mainly employed to analyze protein samples at different stages of the purification process, in order to monitor protein separation and assess sample quality. The use of agarose gel allowed the visualization of biomolecules after migration, helping to verify the presence of the target protein and to evaluate the effectiveness of the extraction and purification steps.

Western blot

Western blot was used during practical laboratory sessions in purification and analysis of proteins to detect and analyze specific proteins after extraction and purification. This technique allowed the separation of proteins by size, their transfer onto a membrane, and their specific detection using antibodies. Western blotting was useful to confirm the presence of the target protein, evaluate its expression level, and assess the effectiveness of the purification process.
