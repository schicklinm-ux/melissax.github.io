Professional experiences

Receptionist and cashier at the Centre Nautique Pierre de Coubertin

(Saint-Louis, 68)

During the last summers, I worked as cashier at the Centre Nautique Pierre de Coubertin in Saint-Louis (Alsace, France), near Basel. I had to answer the customers' questions, give them tickets in order to enter the pool.

This summerwork was very beneficial for me. It was my first step in the working world. As I began, I was very shy and afraid of people. But the more time I spent there, the more I became confident and was not shy anymore. I became un pilier and the customers were adoring me.

I always had a smile and I always did the best to satisfy the customers.
